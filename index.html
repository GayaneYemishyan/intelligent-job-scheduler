How It Works

Three Simple Steps

You submit your tasks. The system handles everything else.

📋
01

Submit Your Tasks

Add tasks with a name, priority level, and any dependencies. For example: "Task B can only start after Task A finishes." No technical knowledge needed — just describe your work.

🧠
02

The AI Decides the Order

The scheduler instantly figures out the smartest sequence. High-priority tasks go first, dependencies are respected, and no task ever gets permanently ignored — even low-priority ones.

🚀
03

Jobs Run on the Best Machine

The system picks the most cost-effective server available. It even predicts problems before they happen and routes jobs around them — automatically.
